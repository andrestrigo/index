Club Deportivo Badminton Antofagasta is characterized by a friendly atmosphere to enjoy playing badminton. There are players of different levels so you always can find a new challenge to improve your skills. Moreover, there are IBF certified coaches and professionals that can help you to find what you are looking for whether it is a new group of friends to have a good time or to improve your skills to participate in different regional, national and international tournaments.

Club Deportivo Antofagasta is known for helping in the coordination of county, state and national tournaments, and it encourages all its members to participate
