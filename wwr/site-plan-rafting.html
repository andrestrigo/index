Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of this website is to attract clients. In orther to accomplish it it will provide all the information related to White Water Rafting services for all users that would like to enjoy river rafting adventures in Colorado. It should include detailed information regarding locations, prices, duration, gear, level of expertise, etc. Clients pay for White Whater Rafting services and with a website it is possible to reach out more clients that help to support the company.

Audience

The website will primarily target people searching for outdoor activities in Colorado. Usually, they will be young individuals or young groups of people ranging between ages between 12-50 years old with an adventurous spirit. Our fonts and colors have to provide a feeling of adventure, fun and safety. So, we have a fan font for our titles and a more formal to provide the information. Moreover, the colors include yellow that usually is associated with fun and optimism and blue that can be associated to trustworthy. The audience will access the site using mobile phone or laptop
